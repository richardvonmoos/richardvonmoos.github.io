I am a doctoral student in computer science at the University of Fribourg,
where I am part of the [AIDOS Lab](https://aidos.group/) under the supervision
of [Professor Bastian Rieck](https://bastian.rieck.me/).

During my studies, I focused on topics in machine learning, topology and graph theory, where 
I applied topological data analysis to the space of outdoor images and analyzed the time-varying
graph structure of the Swiss railway network.

In my research, I look into topological and geometric methods for graph
machine learning and mathematically interpretable representations, but
also into machine learning as a tool for problems in mathematics.
At the moment, I am especially interested in generalization in graph neural
networks and its connections to foundation models.
